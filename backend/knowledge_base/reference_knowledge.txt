### **Section 1: Serviceable Industries & Sectors**
The system identifies and approves tenders within these specific industries:
* **Primary Focus:** Oil & Gas, Petrochemical, Refinery, Pipeline.
* **Infrastructure & Transport:** Metro, Mono Rail, Railways, Infrastructure.
* **Energy & Power:** Power, Renewable Energy, CGD (City Gas Distribution), CNG.
* **Industrial & Technical:** Cement, Fertilizer, Metals & Minerals, EPC (Engineering, Procurement, Construction).
* **Services:** Engineering, IT, Telecom.

### **Section 2: Explicit Decision Rules (Gatekeeper Logic)**
A tender must pass these **Hard Filters** to be considered a "GO":
1.  **Sector Match:** Must belong to one of the 19 industries listed above.
2.  **Service Match:** Must require Manpower Supply, Technical Staffing, O&M, or PMC.
3.  [cite_start]**Banned List Check:** If the client is not a recognized PSU/Public Listed Company or is on Aarvi’s internal "Negative List," it is an automatic **"NO-GO"**[cite: 1349, 2164].
4.  [cite_start]**PQ Mandatory:** Aarvi must satisfy the financial requirement of having successfully completed similar work in the last 3 years[cite: 1952, 4270].

### **Section 3: The Weighted Scoring System (1-100)**
Tenders are scored based on the following weights:
* **Financial PQ (30 pts):** 30 pts if we have 1 PO > 1.38 Cr; [cite_start]20 pts if we have 2 POs > 86.25 Lac[cite: 1952, 3980].
* **Service Category Match (25 pts):** 25 pts for "Highly Skilled" roles (TA, Analyst); [cite_start]15 pts for semi-skilled[cite: 1731, 2984].
* [cite_start]**Client Reputation (20 pts):** 20 pts for Tier-1 PSUs (BPCL, HPCL, IOCL, ONGC)[cite: 1719, 3088].
* **Location Focus (15 pts):** 15 pts for Eastern/Western Regions; [cite_start]5 pts for remote/unfamiliar regions[cite: 2066, 3435].
* **Contract Longevity (10 pts):** 10 pts for 24+ months; [cite_start]5 pts for <12 months[cite: 1678, 3047].

### **Section 5: Negative Filters (The "Red Flags")**
The AI will reject or flag for manual review if:
* [cite_start]**Payment Terms:** Payment cycle mentioned exceeds **60 days** (Aarvi standard is 7-21 days)[cite: 199, 1862].
* [cite_start]**Penalty Clause:** Liquidated Damages (LD) exceed **10% of total contract value** (Aarvi standard is 5%)[cite: 843, 4170].
* [cite_start]**EMD Restrictions:** Tenders requiring EMD from MSE-registered firms (MSEs are legally exempt)[cite: 389, 1261].
* [cite_start]**Liability:** Tenders requiring "Unlimited Liability" (Aarvi standard is 100% of PO value)[cite: 579, 889].
